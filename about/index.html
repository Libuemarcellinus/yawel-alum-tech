Who We Are

Yawel Alum Tech is a registered construction company based in Lagos, Nigeria, specializing in premium aluminum and glass works. We serve real estate developers and construction companies with professional, reliable, and innovative building solutions.

Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and use of high-quality materials have made us a trusted partner for commercial and residential projects across Lagos.

With a team of highly skilled professionals and years of industry experience, we deliver exceptional results that stand the test of time.
